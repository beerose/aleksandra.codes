## Abstract

In this talk, we will dive into the world of TypeScript, exploring how Effect
can significantly enhance our coding experience. We'll compare standard
TypeScript coding with Effect-enhanced versions, showcasing improvements in type
safety and functional programming through practical examples. This presentation
will demonstrate the benefits of adding Effect.ts to your TypeScript toolkit!
