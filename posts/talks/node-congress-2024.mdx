## Abstract

Let's explore how new databases are reshaping developer experiences by
emphasizing type safety. In this talk, we'll see how to integrate EdgeDB with
your favourite JavaScript frameworks, add authentication in just minutes, and
achieve type-safe database interactions without the hassle of an ORM.
